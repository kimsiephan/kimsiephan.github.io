Infinite Sums [PDF]

This project explores the theory of infinite sums, beginning with finite and unordered sums and progressing to convergence criteria. It presents fourteen convergence tests, including comparison, ratio, and root tests, with illustrative examples. The work concludes with advanced topics like series rearrangements, infinite products, and summability methods.

Group Isomorphisms, Quotient Groups, and Fundamental Theorem of Homomorphisms [PDF]

This project covers fundamental concepts in abstract algebra such as group isomorphisms, quotient groups, and homomorphisms. It includes key results like Cayley’s theorem and explores structural properties and real-world applications. The work emphasizes how abstract concepts simplify complex group behavior and reveal deep mathematical connections.
