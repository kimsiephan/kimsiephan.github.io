About me

I am currently an MSc student in Mathematics and Computing at Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad (IIT ISM Dhanbad), and a team member of Cosine Lab of IIT ISM Dhanbad under the supervision of Dr. Tamoghna Ojha, Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics and Computing.

I completed my bachelor's in Mathematics (Hons.) at Kirori Mal College, University of Delhi, India in 2024. During my study, I finished a small project on DYNAMICS OF THE LORENZ CURVE under the supervision of Mr. Vishal Dhawan, Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics.

I have been receiving teaching training, graduating at Regional Teacher Training Centre, Kandal in 2020 to serve as a civil servant under Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport. In 2021, I was a mathematics teacher at Samdach Akak Moha Thoma Pouthisal Chea Sim Prek Anhchan High School (New Generation). In 2018, I completed high school diploma at Bun Rany Hun Sen High School, R'lea Bier.
