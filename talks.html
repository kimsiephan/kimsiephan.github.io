29 May 2025
Presentation

Introduction to Federated Learning [PDF]

Mathematics Association of Cambodia (MAC)
Online Seminar, Dhanbad, India

This webinar introduces Federated Learning (FL), a centralized approach that keeps data local while training models collaboratively. We explore FL workflow, client-server optimization techniques, and challenges like data heterogeneity. Using CIFAR-10 with the Flower framework, we present results, applications, and future directions. The poster can be found here.

Presentation

Gradient Descent Algorithm [PDF]

Mathematics Association of Cambodia (MAC)
Online Seminar, Delhi, India

This talk presents the foundational theory behind the Gradient Descent (GD) algorithm used in optimization. It covers motivation, step size analysis, and convergence under convex settings. Applications in machine learning, such as logistic regression, are also discussed. The poster can be found here.
