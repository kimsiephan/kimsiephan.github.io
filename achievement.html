Dynamics of the Lorenz Curve [PDF]

Kirori Mal College, University of Delhi, 2024

This project explores the Lorenz system as a key example of chaos theory in dynamic systems. It investigates bifurcation behavior and how small changes in initial conditions lead to drastic outcomes. Through theoretical insights and computational experiments, the sensitivity and complexity of the system are analyzed. The study highlights the unpredictability and chaotic nature of the Lorenz curve with potential real-world implications.
